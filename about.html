Angie Lee

Creator  ·  Echoes of Asia

Hello! My name is Angie Lee and I am a high school student. I built this website in hopes of helping people understand more about Asian instruments. As someone who lives in America, I was limited to only seeing western instruments like the violin or piano in my daily life. However, this made me unaware of my history and the fact that my Asian heritage had our own unique instruments.


From this website, I hope to help many users understand that our culture has its own instruments that don't have nearly enough recognition compared to instruments like the piano that many Asian American children play today. I created this interactive website to allow users to find out the history of these instruments as well as get the chance to play them — in the comfort of your home!
